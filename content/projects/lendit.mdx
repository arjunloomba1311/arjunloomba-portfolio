LendIt enables UCLA students to borrow and lend items on campus. It allows borrowers to avoid wasteful purchases, and lenders to
make money off their unused items, a win win! Built with Flutter, Firebase, and Google Cloud Platform. LendIt uses Ripple's XRPL ledger to store and verify transactions. Currently in Beta Testing.
![LendIt App](https://d112y698adiu2z.cloudfront.net/photos/production/software_photos/002/260/339/datas/original.png)

Built an IOT smart locker prototype as a model for a potential physical marketplace.
